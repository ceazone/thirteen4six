## Business Goals

### Primary Goal
Increase occupancy from under 40% to **70%+ within 90 days**, matching or exceeding the DC market median.

### Revenue Targets
| Timeframe | Conservative | Target | Stretch |
|-----------|-------------|--------|---------|
| Monthly (current) | ~$1,400 | $2,800 | $3,500 |
| Monthly (after optimization) | $2,500 | $3,800 | $5,000+ |
| Annual run rate | $30,000 | $45,000 | $60,000+ |

> **Benchmark:** DC 1-bedroom median ADR is $146/night at 68% occupancy = ~$36K/year. Top 25% of DC listings earn $267+/night.

### Secondary Goals
- Achieve Superhost status (requires 4.8+ rating, 90%+ response rate, 10+ stays/year)
- Build a base of repeat guests and direct bookings over time
- Minimize time spent on operations through automation

## Phase 3 — Optimization (Month 2–3)

**Goal:** Use data to refine pricing and identify gaps.

### Monthly Review Process
Every 4 weeks, review:
1. Occupancy rate vs. target for the month
2. Average nightly rate vs. market benchmark
3. Guest reviews — look for patterns in feedback
4. Nights with no bookings — were they priced too high?
5. PriceLabs dashboard — adjust base price ±$10 based on performance

### Pricing Rules to Implement
- **Gap fill rule:** If a 1-2 night gap exists between bookings, drop price by 20% to fill it
- **Last-minute rule:** Reduce price 15% for nights within 3 days (auto-set in PriceLabs)
- **Far-out premium:** Increase price 10% for nights booked 60+ days in advance
- **Weekend premium:** Always set Fri/Sat at 30–50% above your weeknight base

### Listing Refresh
- Update description to reference current season/upcoming events
- Refresh photo order — lead photo matters most for click-through rate
- Update your local guidebook with seasonal recommendations

## Phase 4 — Scale (Month 3+)

**Goal:** Maximize revenue per available night and explore additional income streams.

### Advanced Strategies
- **Mid-term rental play:** In slow months (Jan–Feb), list on Furnished Finder or Zillow for 30-day rentals at $2,200–2,800/month — more predictable than STR in low season
- **Direct booking:** After building a guest database, offer repeat guests a 10% discount to book direct (via Lodgify or Hospitable) — saves you 3% Airbnb fee
- **VRBO cross-listing:** List on VRBO in addition to Airbnb — some guests only search there; increases exposure at no extra cost
- **Corporate housing:** Contact DC staffing agencies and government contractors about furnished monthly rentals — can command $3,000–3,500/month

**Recommended title:**
> *Modern 1BR Near Metro | Private Entrance · Kitchen · Laundry*

**Alternative A (remote work angle):**
> *Work-From-DC Suite | Private Entrance · Fast WiFi · Kitchen · Laundry*

**Alternative B (experience angle):**
> *Columbia Heights Hideaway | Private Suite · Kitchen · Instant Camera · Laundry*

> **Note:** Property is at 1346 Oak St NW (Columbia Heights/Park View), near Columbia Heights Metro (0.4 mi). Do NOT use "Capitol Hill" — that's a different neighborhood entirely.

### Description Framework

**Opening hook (2–3 sentences):** Lead with the experience, not the specs.
> "Work from home, then walk to the Capitol. This private basement suite has everything you need to live like a local in Washington — your own entrance, a fully stocked kitchen, fast WiFi, and laundry in-unit."

**Amenity story (bullet list):** Frame amenities as guest benefits.
- Private entrance — come and go on your schedule
- Full kitchen with dishwasher — cook in and save, or prep before a night out
- Keurig coffee maker + tea, sugar, creamer provided — mornings are covered
- In-unit washer/dryer — perfect for longer stays (detergent provided)
- Fast WiFi (QR code scan to connect) + dedicated desk — remote work ready
- Smart TV with Netflix, Amazon Prime already signed in
- Kodak Printomatic instant camera — snap memories, prints are stickers (our gift to you!)
- Iron & ironing board, blow dryer, charging cables at nightstand
- Black makeup towels provided — no worries about staining white linens
- First aid kit and hydrogen peroxide in bathroom cabinet

**Location section:** 0.4 miles from Columbia Heights Metro (Green/Yellow Lines) — direct access to downtown DC and the National Mall. Walking distance to Meridian Hill Park, U Street corridor, Smithsonian National Zoo (1.2 mi), and Rock Creek Park.

**Nearby food & drink highlights (from our Welcome Guide):**

## Guest Experience & Automation

### 5 Automated Messages to Set Up

**Message 1 — Booking Confirmation** *(send immediately after booking)*
> "Hi [Name]! So excited to host you. Your reservation at [Property] is confirmed for [dates]. I'll send check-in details 3 days before arrival. In the meantime, feel free to ask me anything. Can't wait to have you!"

**Message 2 — Pre-Arrival** *(send 3 days before check-in)*
> "Hi [Name]! Getting excited for your arrival on [date]. Here's what you need to know: [Check-in time, access code/key instructions, parking info, WiFi password]. Full instructions will also be in the Airbnb app. Let me know if you have any questions!"

**Message 3 — Check-In Confirmation** *(send day of check-in, 1 hour after check-in time)*
> "Hi [Name]! Hope you arrived safely and are settling in. The WiFi password is [password], and the TV remote is on the coffee table. Let me know if anything is unclear — I'm just a message away!"

**Message 4 — Mid-Stay Check-In** *(send on day 2 of stay)*
> "Hi [Name]! Just checking in — hope everything is going well! Is there anything you need or anything I can make more comfortable? Happy to help. Enjoy DC!"

**Message 5 — Checkout & Review Request** *(send morning of checkout)*
> "Hi [Name]! Today's your last day — hope you had a wonderful stay! Checkout is by [time]. Please leave the key [instructions]. If you enjoyed your stay, a review would mean the world to me — it helps future guests find the listing. Safe travels, and I hope to host you again!"
