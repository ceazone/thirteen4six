Step-by-Step Setup

Since you already have your PriceLabs account, here's exactly how to configure each setting. These numbers are tailored to your DC 1BR listing and playbook targets.

1

Connect Your Airbnb Listing

In PriceLabs, go to Listings → Add Listings → Airbnb. You'll authorize via Airbnb's official API. Once connected, PriceLabs will import your calendar and existing bookings. Make sure you connect the "Private DC Suite · Free Parking · 9 Min to Metro" listing (and your second listing if applicable).

2

Set Your Base Price

This is the most important setting. PriceLabs uses your base price as the anchor — it adjusts up and down from here based on demand, seasonality, day of week, etc.

Recommended base price: $155/night

Why $155: Your current average is $143 (too low). The DC 1BR median is $146. A $155 base gives PriceLabs room to push weeknights down to ~$130–140 while letting weekends and peak dates rise to $200–300+. PriceLabs' algorithm recommends a "Market-Driven" base — check what it suggests, but if it's below $145, override it to $155.

3

Set Min and Max Prices

These are your guardrails. Without a minimum, PriceLabs can drop your price to fill gaps — sometimes too low.

Setting Value Why
Minimum price $115/night Floor for slow weeknights (Jan/Feb). Covers your costs and cleaning overhead.
Maximum price $400/night Ceiling for once-in-a-generation events like the 250th. Don't cap yourself too low — let the market decide.
4

Set Your Cleaning Fee

Your CSV shows cleaning fees ranging from $50–$100. Make sure PriceLabs knows your cleaning fee so it factors it into the total guest price. In PriceLabs: Listing Settings → Cleaning Fee. Set it to whatever you currently charge on Airbnb (looks like $65 for 2-night stays, $75 for 3+ nights).

5

Enable Sync to Airbnb

Go to Sync Settings and enable automatic price sync. PriceLabs will push prices to your Airbnb calendar daily (or more frequently on some plans). Make sure "Auto-sync" is ON so you don't have to manually push prices.

Advanced Customizations

PriceLabs Rules to Set Up

These rules go under Pricing Customizations in PriceLabs. They handle the edge cases that make a big difference in revenue.

Rule Setting Why It Matters
Weekend adjustment Fri/Sat +35% Your bookings show almost no weekend premium. DC weekends command significantly more, especially during events. This is probably your single biggest revenue lever.
Last-minute discount -15% within 3 days Better to fill a night at 85% of target than leave it empty. PriceLabs can auto-drop prices for unbooked nights within 72 hours.
Far-out premium +10% for 60+ days out Guests booking far ahead are planners willing to pay more. Captures more from early bookers while you can still drop later if needed.
Orphan day pricing -20% for 1–2 night gaps If a 1–2 night gap opens between bookings, drop the price to fill it. Empty nights earn $0.
Occupancy-based adjustment Raise prices when occupancy >70% for the month If your month is filling up fast, remaining nights should cost more. PriceLabs can auto-increase by 5–15% based on how booked you are.
Length-of-stay discount 7 nights: -20%
28 nights: -35%		 Matches your playbook. Incentivizes interns, contractors, and extended-stay guests — your sweet spot in DC.
Dynamic min stay Enable PriceLabs' min stay engine will auto-lower min nights to fill short gaps and raise them during peak periods. Saves you from manually managing this.
